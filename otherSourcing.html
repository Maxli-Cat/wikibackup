General Sourcing guide

Keeping a list of easy places for sourcing non-hormone ingredients 

 

  • Distilled Water
    • Most grocery stores will have in gallon jugs
  • Ethanol
    • When looking, proof = Â½% (ie 80 proof = 40% ethanol, 120 proof = 60%)
    • Places that sell liquor should have everclear or similar, letting you get pure ethanol up to 60% or so
    • Many places will sell denatured ethanol. DO NOT BUY THIS. Denatured means it has been made toxic 
    • Scientific suppliers will often sell 200 proof non-denatured, although they may require various forms of identification since alcohol is age restricted 
  • Isopropyl alcohol 
    • Can often be found 70%-97% at pharmacies and groceries 
      • Currently supplies are still limited due to the pandemic 
    • Can be found easily up to 99.97% online on amazon and similar 
  • Octisalate
    • Can be found on ebay for small quantities
    • If you want a lot scientific suppliers can have better prices if the volume is high enough
