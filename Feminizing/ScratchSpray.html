Estradiol Spray

This is one of the simplest recopies and should be very effective

It's based off the Lenzetto spray

 

Production

Materials

  • Estradiol
  • octisalate
  • ethanol
    • 65%-70% ideal
    • Water down with distilled water if yours is higher
  • spray bottle

 

Process

Making this is very straightforward

  • You want to get 5g of octisalate per 1g of estradiol
  • Don't use more than 20mg estradiol per 1mL of ethanol

 

To make something functionally identical to off the shelf sprays, mix

  • estradiol: .17 g
  • octisalate: .85 g
  • ethanol: 8.98 ml

Which gives 1.7mg per mL

This can have the concentration scaled up, keeping the estradiol and octisalate in proportion and reducing the ethanol to keep the final result at 100mL

And like all our formulas, it can be scaled up or down for a larger or smaller batch

 

Use

Straightforward, spray on skin and leave that section alone until it drys. Note that additional successive sprays on the same patch of skin doesn't linearly increase dosage.1 That means if you need more than one spray you should either mix a higher strength spray or use 2 patches of skin (ie both arms). It seems it's best to wait at least 6 hours between spraying the same spot
