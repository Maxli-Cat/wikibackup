Scratch Gel

This taken from the formula described in this patent

Materials

Reusables

  • Scale
    • Should be able to measure at least 10g ± 0.001g
  • Liquid measure
    • Should be able to measure 100mL ± 0.01mL
    • Can be multiple (ie one that measures 100mL ± 1mL and another that measure 10mL ± 0.01mL)
  • Mixing equpment

Consumables

  • Estradiol
  • Ethanol
    • Isopropyl would likely work as well, but it untested
  • Carbomer 980
  • Triethanolamine 
  • Distilled water

 

Procedure

  1. Mix 0.12g of estradiol into 59mL of ethanol
  2. Mix until estradiol is fully dissolved
  3. Add 1g of carbomber 980 and 1.35g triethanolamine to the mix
  4. Add water to bring the total mixture to 100mL
    1. Should be about 38.5mL
  5. Mix well
