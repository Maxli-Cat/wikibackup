Basic Tincture

The idea is straightforward, since estradiol dissolves in ethanol, and you can drink ethanol, just drink your estradiol

 

Process

High concentration

Making your tincture is very straightforward, get high proof drinking alcohol, 120 proof (60%) like everclear is best although as low as 80 proof (40%) like vodka would likely work.

Measure out your liquid and your estradiol, 1g of E per 100mL of drink. Mix until dissolved. 

To use, take 0.1mL of liquid per 1mg of E wanted. It's best to use something like a lab syringe or an insulin syringe to measure amounts this small. Squirt it under your tongue and don't swallow for a few minutes, and avoid eating/drinking/rinsing for a while

Note that dosing amounts here are sublingual, not transdermal 

 

Low concentration

Making your tincture is very straightforward, get high proof drinking alcohol, 120 proof (60%) like everclear is best although as low as 80 proof (40%) like vodka would likely work.

Measure out your liquid and your estradiol, 1mg of E per 1mL of drink. Mix until dissolved. 

To use, take 0.1mL of liquid per 1mg of E wanted. Best to use something like a feeding syringe or a children's medication syringe for measuring. Squirt it under your tongue and don't swallow for a few minutes, and avoid eating/drinking/rinsing for a while

Note that dosing amounts here are sublingual, not transdermal
