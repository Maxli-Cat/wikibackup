Sourcing Advice

Estradiol

You want to get 17 beta-estradiol powder (CAS 50-28-2). This is easy enough to find on alibaba, and you can usually get somewhere between $1-$5, plus shipping.

In the US, this is not a controlled substance, so there shouldn't be any legal or customs issues. In general, you should check your local laws rather than just trusting online strangers.
